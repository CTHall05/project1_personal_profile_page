My Story

At the age of 21 I had finished my Film and Television Production Degree and I had no direction except for this deep urge to travel around the world and meet everyone that I could. I managed to obtain a role as a Cabin Crew for the world class airline Emirates. I tasted food, danced with cultures, drank experiences I never thought possible and made friends for life. After 3 and a half years of adventure I felt that I had to try something new. Whilst my original plans for 2020 were completely turned upside down I stumbled across a video about Software Development and I was hooked. I typed my first bit of HTML at the age of 27 and right now I am excited to dive into the Front End Development TechDegree Program that Team Treehouse is providing!

Currently I am enrolled on the Team Treehouse Tech Degree program, learning Front End Development. Which involves learning HTML, CSS and Javascript. My projects from this program will be readily viewable on my Github. Please have a look to see my latest work.
